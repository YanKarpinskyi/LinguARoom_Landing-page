ABOUT US

LinguARoom isn't just about learning — it's about real conversations with a virtual language partner.

We're building an AI-powered app that helps you practice any language in real-life situations — from a café chat to a job interview.

With us you can

Talk in real time

Speak with an AI partner who responds by voice and text — just like a real conversation.

Get instant feedback

AI analyzes your pronunciation, grammar, and vocabulary, then gives quick tips for improvement.

Choose your scene and situation

Practice in VR/AR environments or just in the app — from a café to a job interview or an airport.

Change your conversation partners

Select speakers of different ages, nationalities, and communication styles.

Pick your topic and level

Learn through stories
